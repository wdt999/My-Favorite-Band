 Spyair is a Japanese rock band from Nagoya, formed in 2005.  The band currently consists of Yuji "UZ" Nakai (guitar, programming), Kenji "Momiken" Momiyama (bass guitar, leader), Kenta Sasabe (drums) and Yosuke (vocals).  The band's name was determined in one shot by the members, which came from the word spyware, a type of computer virus.
