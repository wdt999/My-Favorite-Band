 DEXCORE was formed on October 28, 2016.

 Most of their music is based on metal core, but they try to harmonize the melody with a pop approach symmetrical to metal sound. They are characterized by vocals and intense breakdowns that go beyond clean and scrimming.  They consist of vocals -kagami-, guitar -yumeto-, bass -TO-RU-, and support drummer MAKI.
